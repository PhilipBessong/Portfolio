The Experience

Ladies and Gentlemen, welcome to Seb's Elite Boxing. This isn't your ordinary faceless gym like the name says, it's an Elite Boxing Gym. That means great workouts, progressive learning, and hard-earned rewards in a motivating environment.

Most members, men, women, boys, and girls don't do full contact boxing but enjoy one-on-one coaching or high-energy fitness classes. They leave with improved conditioning, confidence, and a smile. And let's face it, punching pads is better stress relief than shouting at the kids, kicking the dog, or hammering the credit card!

The Seb's Experience

Don't mistake us for some sterile health club and don't come here if you seek the mere facade of fitness. If your goal is to get into great shape and you've got the motivation, then you've come to the right place. By mixing Boxing and Physical Training classes, you'll cover all six S's of peak boxing fitness (Speed, Stamina, Strength, Suppleness, Skill & pSychology. Yes, we cheated on the last one).

We'll teach you how to use your fists to defend and attack, and we'll get you into great condition. But beyond that, we'll help build your confidence so you walk out with a spring in your step and a smile on your face. Sebs attracts people with character, those willing to explore their primal side and discover what they're truly made of.

It's not about knocking lumps out of others, but about finding out whether you have what it takes when the pressure is on. Paradoxically, while boxing is an individual sport, Sebs is also very communal camaraderie and encouragement flow naturally here.

Our classes are for anyone and everyone serious about fitness. From out of shape to peak condition, we've got you covered. And don't worry, you don't have to fight in the ring to train like a fighter. We'll help you build confidence, grit, and a work ethic that carries into every part of life.
