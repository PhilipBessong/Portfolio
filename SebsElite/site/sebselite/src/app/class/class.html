Personal Training

If you're looking for a bespoke experience, our Personal Training at Seb's Elite Boxing is where champions are made. This is one-on-one coaching designed specifically for your fitness level and goals. You get the undivided attention of an elite trainer, ensuring your technique is perfect and your progress is accelerated.

Our PT sessions focus on high-intensity pad work, technical drills, and strength conditioning. It is the ultimate way to sharpen your skills without the pressure of a group. And just like our classes, it's the world's best stress relief—nothing clears the mind quite like a focused 45-minute session on the pads after a long day at the office.

Whether you are training for a specific goal, coming back from an injury, or simply prefer the privacy of individual coaching, our personal training hours (10am - 2pm) provide the perfect elite environment to push your limits and see real, measurable results.
