About The Plum

Discover a sanctuary of modern elegance in the heart of Louis Trichardt. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, we provide a comfortable home-away-from-home at the foot of the Soutpansberg mountains.

Modern Luxury Meets Comfort

The Plum, as we affectionately call our home, is nestled in a quiet residential neighbourhood in Louis Trichardt (Makhado), at the foot of the scenic Soutpansberg Mountains. Designed for professionals, business travelers, and discerning individuals, The Plum offers a peaceful retreat without sacrificing convenience.

Powered primarily by solar energy and supported by the national grid, The Plum ensures a reliable, uninterrupted power supply ideal for work, relaxation, and everyday comfort. Guests are also perfectly positioned to enjoy nearby attractions, including the Hanglip Forest Reserve, Louis Trichardt Golf Club, and Ben Lavin Nature Reserve.
